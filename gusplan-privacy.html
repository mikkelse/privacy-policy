Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 7, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Gusplan, which displays the weekly sauna gus plan schedule.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users of the app.

However, we use third-party services provided by Google Firebase for the following purposes:

  • Firebase Analytics: To collect anonymous usage statistics such as screen views, user engagement, and device information, helping us understand how the app is used.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To collect crash reports and error logs to improve the app’s stability and performance.

The information collected is non-personal and cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use the Information

The anonymous data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Understand user behavior and engagement
  • Diagnose technical issues and crashes

We do not share or sell any data and we do not use the data for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

All analytics and crash data are retained by Firebase in accordance with Google’s standard data retention policies. We do not store any user data on our own servers.

Your Consent

By using the Gusplan app, you consent to the collection and use of anonymous data as described in this Privacy Policy.
