Privacy Policy

This app is a companion to a music festival and provides users with event schedules, artist descriptions, news updates, and general festival information.

Data Collection

The app does not collect or store any personal information from users. It does not request or track location data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase services for the following purposes:

  • Cloud Firestore – to store and deliver festival content such as schedules and artist info.
  • Cloud Messaging – to send notifications about important updates.
  • Crashlytics – to report app crashes and improve stability.

No personally identifiable information is collected or transmitted through these services.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please visit https://www.acolossalweekend.dk for contact information.
